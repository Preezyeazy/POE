About Us

The Maboneng Youth Arts Collective is an artistic initiative that uses spoken words, dance, music, visual arts and street theatre to uplift young people in Johannesburgâ€™s inner city.

Founded in 2010, the collective has been a beacon of hope and creativity for young artists in the Maboneng Precinct. Our mission is to empower youth through the arts, providing them with a platform to express themselves and develop their talents.

To create a vibrant community where young artists can thrive, collaborate, and contribute to the cultural landscape of Johannesburg.
